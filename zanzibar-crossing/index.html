Live Tracking: them vs us

1
Open the My Map in the Google Maps app (Saved → Maps) so the route line shows on everyone's phone.
2
Runner shares live location (Google Maps → profile → Location sharing, or WhatsApp live location) with the crew, set to "until I turn off".
3
Crew opens that shared location in Google Maps. The runner's blue dot appears on the same map as the route line, so you see distance to the next aid stop at a glance.
4
Backup: match the runner's blue dot to the ETA table (page 2) and your car odometer between stops.

Car Box

  • Cooler + ice, frozen wet towels/buffs
  • Box of water + small bottles (ice some)
  • Pre-mixed syrup flasks, ready to grab
  • Spare shoes + socks (plural) + clothes
  • First aid: disinfectant, irritation cream, anti-chafe stick, blister tape, scissors, needle, plasters
  • Bug spray, sunscreen + SPF lip balm
  • Nutrition: simple syrup, haluwa, salt pills, chapati + PB, bananas, coke
  • Flip flops, wet wipes, toilet paper, bin bag
  • Power bank, cables, spare batteries
  • Spare head torch (full backup)
  • Reflective vest + red blinker light

On-Body Vest

  • 2 x 500 ml flasks (water + syrup/coke)
  • 2–3 gels backup
  • Salt pills in ziplock
  • Phone: offline map + live location on
  • Cash, ID / emergency card, whistle
  • Anti-chafe mini, few plasters, TP
  • Day: cap + sunglasses
  • Night: head torch + red blinker
  • Buff (soak to cool)

To Prepare

  • Confirm people to run sections with you
  • Cooler with ice loaded
  • Spare head torch charged
  • Route map on watch (+ phone offline)
  • ID packed
  • Live location shared with crew
  • Brief crew on stop spacing + ETAs
  • Phones + power bank fully charged

Rotate sources so you don't gag: gel on move coke/syrup at stop chapati+PB / haluwa / banana. Skip Naak (bloats). Cold drinks go down easier. Stops under 90 sec.

Heat Plan (first 3.5h)

  • Pour water over head + neck every stop
  • Ice in cap and in buff
  • Keep pace genuinely easy – don't chase early "good legs"
  • Sunscreen at start + 1 reapply
  • Most people overcook the first 30 km. Don't.

Night Safety

  • Reflective + red blinker on after 18:00
  • Car leapfrogs / runs near you on dark roads
  • Live location live, phone charged

Pre-Departure (from 13:30)

  • Cooler iced, water + flasks loaded
  • Pre-mix all syrup flasks
  • Spare head torch + power bank charged
  • Runner's live location shared & tested
  • My Map open on crew phones
  • Leave home 14:00, start 15:00

If Things Go Wrong

  • Stomach off: switch to coke + banana, ease pace, drop gels briefly
  • Overheating: stop, ice neck/groin, cold towel, walk till HR drops
  • Cramps: salt pill + coke, short walk
  • Blisters: tape early, don't wait
  • Car stays reachable; never let gap exceed one stop on dark roads
